Hi, I’m Mau, a former math teacher turned aspiring virtual bookkeeper. I’ve always loved working with numbers, but more than that, I enjoy helping people understand them without the stress or confusion.

My background in teaching trained me to be patient, detail-oriented, and organized—skills I now bring into my bookkeeping work. I’m currently enrolled in a bookkeeping masterclass, where I continue to sharpen my skills in organizing financial records, reconciling accounts, and preparing accurate reports.

As a bookkeeper, my goal is simple: to keep your books clean, organized, and easy to understand so you can focus on running your business with confidence. I believe bookkeeping doesn’t have to feel intimidating. With the right systems and a supportive approach, it can actually feel empowering.

I’m here to be your book bud—someone you can rely on to take care of the numbers and help bring clarity to your business finances and help you focus on growing your business as well.
